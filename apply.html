Application Process

A comprehensive selection process designed to identify and nurture exceptional talent

1

Online Application

Complete the detailed application form with academic records, personal statement, and recommendation letters.

Duration: 1-2 weeks
2

Initial Screening

Review of academic achievements, extracurricular activities, and alignment with program objectives.

Duration: 2-3 weeks
3

Assessment Tests

Online aptitude tests in mathematics, science, and logical reasoning adapted for visually impaired students.

Duration: 1 week
4

Virtual Interview

One-on-one interview with program coordinators to assess motivation, goals, and potential for success.

Duration: 2 weeks
5

Final Selection

Comprehensive evaluation and selection of 30 students for the Vision 30 program.

Duration: 1 week

Eligibility Criteria

Requirements to be part of the Vision 30 program

Visual Impairment

  • Complete blindness or low vision
  • Medical certificate required
  • Comfortable using assistive technology

Academic Excellence

  • Minimum 80% in Class 10 or equivalent
  • Strong performance in mathematics and science
  • Currently enrolled in Class 11 or 12

Residency

  • Indian citizen or permanent resident
  • Currently residing in India
  • Willing to participate in residential camps

Motivation & Commitment

  • Genuine interest in STEM fields
  • Commitment to 3-year program duration
  • Leadership potential and social awareness
