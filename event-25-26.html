Addressing STEM Under-representation

Understanding the barriers that prevent visually impaired students from pursuing STEM careers

Under-representation Crisis

The severe under-representation of people with visual impairment in STEM careers creates a vicious cycle that perpetuates inequality and limits opportunities for social mobility.

Educational Barriers

Limited access to educational opportunities on par with sighted peers directly impacts the realization of UN SDG #4 of quality education for all.

Lack of Role Models

Absence of visible role models and inclusive school environments contributes to continued exclusion from mainstream STEM careers.

Economic Impact

Students lose out on joining India's well-paying IT industry, affecting their economic independence and social mobility.

Vision 30: A Movement for Change

More than an educational program - it's a movement towards creating an inclusive society

Holistic Educational Environment

Vision 30 is designed by Vision Empower in partnership with I-Stem to provide a comprehensive and inclusive educational environment for 30 promising blind and low vision students from across India.

Our Long-term Objectives:

  • Long-term societal change where disability is not seen as a barrier to academic and professional success
  • Enhanced opportunities and life outcomes for blind and low vision students, as well as broader disability communities
  • A significant contribution to the movement for inclusive education and equality in India and potentially globally
