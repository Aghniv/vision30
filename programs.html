Program Goals & Objectives

Comprehensive objectives designed to transform STEM education for blind and low-vision students

Academic Excellence

Enable students to achieve academic excellence and gain admission to top colleges and universities through advanced STEM curriculum and personalized learning approaches.

  • Advanced mathematics, physics, chemistry, and computer science mastery
  • University entrance exam preparation (JEE, NEET)
  • Research methodology and scientific writing skills
  • Critical thinking and problem-solving development

Role Model Development

Identify, inspire, and support deserving students to emerge as role models who can inspire and advocate for others with similar challenges.

  • Leadership development and mentorship training
  • Public speaking and advocacy skills
  • Community engagement and outreach programs
  • Peer support network establishment

Confidence & Independence

Develop increased confidence, independence, and social integration to ensure students thrive in academic and professional environments.

  • Assistive technology mastery and independence
  • Life skills and daily living competencies
  • Social integration and communication skills
  • Mental health and wellbeing support

Comprehensive Program Structure

Six integrated pillars supporting holistic student development and STEM excellence

Academic Excellence

Advanced curriculum in mathematics, physics, chemistry, and computer science with specialized teaching methods adapted for blind and low-vision students.

  • Personalized learning plans
  • Advanced problem-solving techniques
  • University entrance exam preparation
  • Research methodology training

Technology Training

Comprehensive training in assistive technologies, programming languages, and digital tools essential for STEM careers.

  • Screen reader proficiency (NVDA, JAWS)
  • Programming languages (Python, Java)
  • Scientific computing tools
  • Data analysis and visualization

Career Development

Professional development workshops, industry connections, and career guidance for successful transition to higher education and careers.

  • Industry mentorship programs
  • Interview and presentation skills
  • Networking opportunities
  • Internship placements

Life Skills & Wellbeing

Holistic development including communication skills, leadership training, and mental health support for overall personal growth.

  • Leadership development
  • Communication and presentation
  • Mental health support
  • Peer support networks

Mentorship Program

One-on-one mentorship with successful professionals in STEM fields who provide guidance, support, and inspiration.

  • Industry expert mentors
  • Regular one-on-one sessions
  • Goal setting and tracking
  • Career pathway guidance

Community Building

Creating a strong network of peers, alumni, and supporters that continues beyond the program duration.

  • Peer learning groups
  • Alumni network access
  • Community events and workshops
  • Ongoing support system

3-Year Program Journey

A structured pathway with clear milestones and progressive skill development

1
Year 1: Foundation & Assessment

Building Strong Foundations

Initial assessment, foundation building in core STEM subjects, introduction to assistive technologies, and basic research skills development.

Key Agenda Items:

  • Comprehensive skill and knowledge assessment
  • Foundation courses in mathematics, physics, chemistry
  • Introduction to assistive technology tools
  • Basic research methodology and scientific writing
  • Peer network establishment and team building
  • Goal setting and individual learning plans

Year 1 Milestones:

Technology Proficiency Certification
First Research Project Completion
Foundation Course Certification
Mentor Assignment & First Meeting
2
Year 2: Advancement & Specialization

Advanced Learning & Industry Exposure

Advanced coursework, specialization selection, industry exposure, competitive exam preparation, and intensive skill development.

Key Agenda Items:

  • Advanced STEM curriculum and specialization tracks
  • Industry internship and real-world project exposure
  • Competitive examination preparation (JEE, NEET, etc.)
  • Advanced programming and data science training
  • Leadership development workshops and seminars
  • Research project implementation and presentation

Specialization Track Selection

Leadership & Career Preparation

University applications, capstone projects, leadership roles, career preparation, and transition to independent success in chosen fields.

Key Agenda Items:

  • University application process and entrance exams
  • Capstone research project and thesis defense
  • Leadership roles in community and advocacy
  • Career guidance and job application preparation
  • Alumni network integration and mentorship transition
  • Public speaking and conference presentations

University Admission Success

Our Partner Organizations

Collaborating with leading institutions to deliver exceptional educational experiences

Vision Empower

A Not-for-Profit enterprise empowering children with visual impairment through inclusive education and comprehensive support systems. Leading the educational framework design for Vision 30.

Key Contributions:

  • Curriculum development and academic support
  • Student assessment and selection process
  • Educational methodology and teaching strategies
  • Ongoing academic mentorship and guidance

I-Stem

The digital inclusion partner for PwDs across life stages, enabling access to information and opportunities at scale with AI technology. Providing technological backbone for Vision 30.

Key Contributions:

  • Assistive technology training and support
  • Digital accessibility solutions development
  • AI-powered learning tools and platforms
  • Technical infrastructure and digital resources

RKM Blind Boys' Academy

Premier educational institution in Narendrapur, Kolkata, providing world-class facilities and hosting our residential learning camps with exceptional support infrastructure.

Key Contributions:

  • Residential camp hosting and facilities
  • Educational infrastructure and resources
  • Student accommodation and dining services
  • Academic collaboration and knowledge sharing
